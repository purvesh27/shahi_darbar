SHAHI DARBAR

SHAHI DARBAR was established in 2021 by team of 4 coders only. Initially, it was named as HUNGERSHOP but in early 2021, it was renamed as SHAHI DARBAR .. It provides not only information related to nearby restaurants but also provides facilities such as online ordering, table reservations and management. SHAHI DARBAR currently serves in 1000 cities across 10 countries having 1.2M popular restaurants with 20 Million foodies every month. It is available in 10 different languages and has 100 million reviews with 18 million bookmarks.. It gives a platform to the restaurants owners to serves a large number of users a good quality of food.
